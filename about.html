The Whole Logistics Solutions Organization

At Grant Port Services, we are committed to providing exceptional shipping services and logistics solutions to our clients. Our focus on customer satisfaction, safety, and reliability sets us apart in the industry. We strive to exceed expectations and build long-lasting partnerships with our clients.

  • we provide our customers with detailed cargo updates
  • We fly single or sophisticated goods to any destination at any time
  • we manage and monitor your goods from loading at your supplier to unloading at your client

Grant Port Services covers every port and safe anchorage across the Nigeria employing qualified staff with a broad range of experience in handling every type of vessel; bulk and breakbulk carriers, oil and gas tankers of all sizes, naval vessels, cable and pipe layers, heavy lift, reefer ships Drilling vessels and Jack-up badges.
