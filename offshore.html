  • Oct 16, 2023

Grant Port Services has successfully and efficiently managed import, export and project cargoes. We have provided and will continue to provide port agency services on vessels that carries all types of cargoes in and out of Nigerian ports.

Our variety of specialised services which includes, customs clearance and distribution, facilitate smooth import and export operations.

All import/export documentations are handled with precision knowing the importance of proper documentation in cargo clearing either for export or import.

We advise clients on Nigerian customs regulations and procedures to enable them take advantage of the best and most cost-effective solutions. Our services are tailored to meet the demand in the oil and gas sector.
