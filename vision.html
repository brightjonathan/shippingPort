vision statement

Grant Port Services was founded on the philosophy of fairness, transparency and cost-effectiveness and every employee is required to work to the principles.

  • Integrity - We are committed to being honest, avoiding conflicts of interest and respecting confidential information and will not to seek to compromise people through inducements or entertainment.
  • Respect - We are committed to respecting the people we work with, the communities we work within and the environment in which we operate.
  • Fairness - We are committed to respecting the people we work with, the communities we work within and the environment in which we operate.

At Grant Port Services, these values are rigorously applied and maintained by highly experienced and resourceful team, to ensure that the focus is on offering optimum customer service.
